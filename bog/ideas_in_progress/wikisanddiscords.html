Frustration

There's this really widespread problem with a lot of fighting games, that I will basically sum up as the "twitter tech" problem. Basically, it can be very difficult to find the answer to certain techniques or to learn what to look for in certain matchups because the information is often hidden inside of some discord somewhere.

Now at first, that doesn't seem horrible. So what if you have to go the Ryu Discord server to learn how Ryu beats Chun-Li?
